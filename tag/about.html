Introduction

Thu 02 October 2014 | tags: introductionabout

The purpose of this blog is to keep track of the stuff I learn about Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. While most of the stuff I post may only make sense to my head, I will try to make them as consistent as possible in the case that someone else reads this. I am still an undergraduate student, therefore I may post erroneous information. If you find such errors, I would appreciate that you let me know, as I want to understand this the best I can. Sometimes I may post code to some other problems that may be unrelated to AI/ML, that just means that I was in the mood for something else. Learning and applying what I learn is my passion, as someone I believe to be my friend made me realize. I vehemently search for the truth behind things, and will go as far as my curiosity may take me.
